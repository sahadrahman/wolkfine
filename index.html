Some common questions
were often asked

Do you redesign existing websites?

We certainly can do! If you are looking for just content changes and graphic changes, then it’s good idea to redesign. But if your site in not hitting industry benchmarks (Site speed, mobile responsiveness, content architecture), then it’s a good time to go for a new site.

How much does a website cost?

It completely depends on what you need, minimum amount of time used to design and custom develop your project. Though our website projects generally start in the range of INR.25,000 for basic websites range upward depending on your unique needs

How soon can my website launch?

Launch date will depend on the complexity of your website. Our average period is 20-30 days for a basic business website. If you have a specific launch date in mind, our project manager will be happy to work with you in setting up a timeline to achieve it.

What kind of Businesses / clients do you work with?

We work with a broad range of company types (Start-ups, SME, Large Corporations, Non-profit organizations, B2C and more) across many business industries (Technology, Food, Travel, Apparel, Health + Beauty, Arts, Fair trade and more)

Are there any other costs for websites?

If you host through us, there are hosting charges. We have a dedicated server and your websites will be hosted securely and with privacy. There are charges for domain if you are designing a new website.

What is web hosting and can I purchase my domain?

Web hosting is required in order to get your website online. It is the space that you ‘rent’ which contains your web files and allows users to access your site. YES, you can purchase your domain (With your credit/ debit card and email id).
